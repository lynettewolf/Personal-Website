My name is Lynette Hardinger and I'm an aspiring software engineer. I haven't always wanted to be a programmer. For a long time, I had dreamed of being an animator and graphic designer. As I was falling in love with computer science, I also fell in love with the man who would become my husband, Kendal. Though our interests are different - he graduated from K-State with a B.S. in Geography - our passion for learning is the same. Whether it's related to computers or geography, Star Wars or animation, we are always looking to learn!
